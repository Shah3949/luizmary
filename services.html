Service 01
General Trading

Operating as a licensed General Trading entity in the UAE's Sharjah Publishing City Free Zone, Luizmary FZE LLC provides businesses with the structure and support to engage in lawful import, export, and domestic trade across a broad spectrum of goods and commodities.

Whether you're an international entrepreneur seeking a UAE trading foothold or an established business expanding your product lines, our General Trading licence covers the full operational breadth required to trade freely and compliantly.

  • Import and export of goods across permitted categories
  • Domestic supply chain and commodity distribution
  • Supplier due diligence and vendor management support
  • Trade documentation preparation and compliance review
  • Free zone trading privileges — 100% foreign ownership
  • Customs coordination and Incoterms advisory
  • Product sourcing and procurement guidance

Why it matters: A properly structured UAE trading licence is the gateway to the wider Middle East, Africa, and South Asia markets. We ensure your entity is set up correctly from day one.

Service 02
E-Commerce & Retail Online

The UAE is one of the fastest-growing e-commerce markets in the MENA region. Luizmary FZE LLC is licensed for Retail Sale via Mail Order Houses or via the Internet (E-Commerce), meaning we help you structure, launch, and run a compliant online retail operation from a UAE Free Zone base.

We guide businesses through the practical and regulatory complexities of selling online in the UAE — from trade licence structures to consumer law compliance and cross-border logistics.

  • E-commerce trade licence setup and advisory
  • Online retail regulatory framework compliance
  • Cross-border digital sales and import duty guidance
  • Payment gateway integration advisory
  • UAE consumer protection law compliance review
  • Marketplace seller compliance (Amazon UAE, Noon, etc.)
  • Returns, refunds, and data protection policy structuring

Key advantage: A Free Zone e-commerce licence in the UAE gives you 0% corporate tax (within the small business relief threshold), 100% profit repatriation, and access to one of the world's most connected logistics hubs.

Service 03
Compliance Consultancy

In today's regulatory environment, compliance is not optional — it is foundational. Luizmary FZE LLC offers a full-service compliance consultancy practice helping businesses in the UAE and beyond build robust compliance frameworks, meet regulatory requirements, and avoid costly penalties.

Our approach is proactive rather than reactive: we help clients understand what compliance obligations apply to their business, implement the right controls, and stay audit-ready at all times.

  • Anti-Money Laundering (AML) & Counter-Terrorism Financing (CFT) compliance programs
  • AML risk assessments and Customer Due Diligence (CDD) framework design
  • Regulatory risk identification, mapping, and gap analysis
  • Compliance policy and procedure development
  • Internal compliance audit and health check
  • Regulatory liaison and authority submission support
  • KYC/KYB process design and implementation
  • Ongoing compliance monitoring and advisory retainer

UAE Context: The UAE's regulatory framework has evolved significantly — including mandatory AML obligations for Designated Non-Financial Businesses and Professions (DNFBPs). We keep you ahead of regulatory change.

Service 04
Accounting, Bookkeeping & Tax Consultancy

Luizmary FZE LLC is licensed to provide accounting, bookkeeping, auditing, and tax consultancy services to UAE-registered businesses and international clients. Our financial services practice combines technical precision with practical commercial insight to ensure your numbers work as hard as your business does.

With the UAE's introduction of Corporate Income Tax (CIT) and ongoing VAT obligations, having expert financial and tax support is no longer a luxury — it's a necessity.

  • Full-cycle bookkeeping and accounts maintenance
  • Monthly, quarterly, and annual financial reporting
  • Statutory audit preparation and coordination
  • Internal audit services and financial health reviews
  • VAT registration, return filing, and refund assistance
  • UAE Corporate Tax (CIT) advisory and structuring
  • Tax residency certificate advisory
  • Transfer pricing documentation and advisory
  • Economic Substance Regulation (ESR) compliance

UAE Tax Landscape: Since the introduction of a 9% Corporate Tax in 2023 and ongoing VAT obligations, accurate financial record-keeping and proactive tax planning are essential for all UAE businesses.

Service 05
Corporate Services Provider

As a licensed Corporate Services Provider (CSP) in the UAE, Luizmary FZE LLC delivers end-to-end corporate support services that take the administrative complexity out of running a UAE business. From initial company registration through to annual renewals, visa management, and document clearance — we handle it all.

Our corporate services are designed for both new market entrants and established businesses seeking a reliable, knowledgeable partner to manage their UAE corporate affairs.

  • Company registration and trade licence setup (Free Zone, Mainland, Offshore)
  • PRO (Public Relations Officer) services and government liaison
  • Residence visa and employment permit facilitation
  • Trade licence renewal and amendment management
  • Document clearing, attestation, and legalisation
  • Corporate bank account opening advisory and facilitation
  • Registered office and business address services
  • Ultimate Beneficial Owner (UBO) registration and filing
  • Company liquidation and deregistration support

Full lifecycle support: From incorporating your business to ongoing annual renewals and regulatory filings, we act as your corporate affairs partner so you can focus on growing your business.
